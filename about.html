About Us

Rooted in the Pacific Northwest, reaching for a global future

Our philosophy is built upon five foundational pillars that guide our approach and shape our students into knowledgeable, responsible, and globally minded individuals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop enlightened, ethical, and compassionate individuals. We empower tomorrow's leaders with creativity, resilience, and critical thinking to inspire meaningful action as global citizens.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to blend local and international cultures, shaping a world transformed by our ethical, and compassionate leaders in the heart of the Pacific Northwest.

Our Core Values

RESPECT INTEGRITY CITIZENSHIP EXCELLENCE

Our Philosophy: The 5 Pillars

Each pillar is a cornerstone of the WA Prep experience.

Academic Excellence

Through the International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP), Project Based Learning, and STEM Programming, we challenge our students on a daily basis to excel beyond academic requirements at the state and national levels.

Multiculturalism

We are a diverse community that recognizes and appreciates cultures through academic endeavors and personal experiences.

Experiential Learning

Our program provides students with experiences that build knowledge, self-confidence, teamwork, leadership, and responsibility.

Entrepreneurial Mindset

The entrepreneurial mindset allows our students to create value by unlocking resilience to overcome uncertainties while seizing opportunity in their respective communities.

Service and Leadership

Our program provides service opportunities that allow for personal growth on each student’s path to becoming a leader.
